Cal-table

I have made a table that acts as a calander using Json and html

For this experiment at first I had to look up ways to orginise the information and then worked on putting the years and months together in order. After I had finished that part I tried to put it into a soft coded table which was hard to do so I looked up different ways for it to show and then picked a few and put it together from that. The most challanging part about this project would be trying to find out how to put the Json information in the correct order, this is because there are many things and options that you can use Json for but not many people know about it or know how to use the tool. In addition it was enjoyable to put together the freedoem project since it was somehting I wanted to make atleast once and at first it did not seem too diffucult for me to make but after a while I realised that it was only something so small of what could be made form the tool.
